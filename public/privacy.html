Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are important to us.

Last Updated: 3 December 2025

WorkDen ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of users ("you", "user") who access or use our website, platform, or related services. By using WorkDen, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, contact number, city/state details, account identifiers.
  • Account & Task Information: Login details, submissions, performance records.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device data, login timestamps, cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Allocate and review tasks
  • Ensure platform security
  • Communicate updates and support
  • Prevent fraud or misuse

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal user data. Information may be shared only with trusted service providers, to comply with legal obligations, or to protect the rights and safety of WorkDen and its users.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect user data. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies may be used to maintain login sessions and improve user experience.

6. User Responsibilities

Users must keep login credentials confidential and provide accurate information. Misuse may result in suspension.

7. Task Data & Uploaded Files

Task-related files are used strictly for verification and quality review. Data is not reused or distributed.

8. Registration Fees & Refund Policy

WorkDen may charge a one-time registration or portal access fee. All registration or access fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.

9. Third-Party Services

We are not responsible for privacy practices of external platforms such as payment gateways or external links.

10. Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the platform and meet legal or audit requirements.

11. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us. Requests will be handled within a reasonable timeframe.

12. Children's Privacy

WorkDen services are intended for users aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of changes.

14. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy shall be governed by the laws of India.
